# SLOWSCAPE Rules

## Ruleset

1. We will do one "thing" per day.

   A "thing" is any action that:

   - has an output;
   - changes our game state, such as gaining experience or using an item; or
   - accomplishes something, such as speaking to an NPC, using a shop, etc.

   A "thing" is not necessarily limited to a single click.

2. Walking, menuing, and emoting do not count as "things."

3. Exceptions to the rules will be made where necessary to complete quests, such as Imp Catcher, or where decided upon by community polling.

## Community Poll: Output Quantity

**Question:** Should we be able to produce any amount of outputs as a single thing for actions like spinning wool, smelting bars, smithing items, etc., or should we be limited to one output per thing?

**Result:**

- Any Amount: 74%
- One Output: 26%

## Current Interpretation

Actions such as spinning wool, smelting bars, smithing items, and similar production actions may produce any amount of outputs as a single "thing."

A qualifying production action is therefore counted by the activity being performed, not by the number of individual outputs produced during that activity.

## Notes

- Walking, menuing, and emoting remain free/non-counted activities.
- Quest-related exceptions may be made where necessary.
- Future community polls may override or clarify edge cases.
